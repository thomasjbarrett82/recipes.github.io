# Pork Rub

## Ingredients

- ½ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup kosher salt
- 1 T. paprika
- 1 T. chili powder
- 1 T. mustard powder
- 1 T. garlic powder
- 1 T. onion powder
- 1 T. black pepper
- 1 T. Italian seasoning
- 1 t. ginger
