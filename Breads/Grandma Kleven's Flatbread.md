# Grandma Kleven's Flatbread

Source: Judy Munson

## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter, melted
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 2 cups flour

## Prep

- Mix ingredients together and put in fridge overnight
- Heat oven to 350 F

## Directions

- Roll in flour until thin
- Bake on cookie sheet until browned
- Cut while still warm
