# Frozen Corn

Source: Grandma Judy Munson

## Ingredients

- 16 cups fresh corn, cut off the cob
- 3 cups water
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp. kosher salt

## Directions

- Stir all ingredients in a large bowl
- Separate into containers and freeze
