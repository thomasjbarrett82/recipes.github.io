# Chicken Tenders

Source: Judy

## Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts
- 2 tbsp. mayo
- 4 tbsp. Shake & Bake

## Prep

- Cut chicken into tenders or nuggets
- Heat air fryer to 375 F

## Directions

- Add chicken and mayo to large ziploc bag
- Shake to fully coat chicken
- Add Shake & Bake and shake to fully coat
- Air fry for 7-8 minutes on each side
