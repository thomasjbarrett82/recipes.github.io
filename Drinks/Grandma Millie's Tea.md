# Grandma Millie's Tea

Source: Judy Munson

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Tang
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup instant black tea
- 1 package unsweetened lemonade mix
- 1 tsp. cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp. nutmet

## Prep

- Mix all ingredients and store in airtight container

## Directions

- Mix to taste in a cup of hot water
